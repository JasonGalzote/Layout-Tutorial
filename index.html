The Black Death

The Bubonic Plague or Black Death was originated from the "Death Ships" that was pulled into the port at Messina, Many Europeans heard rumors about the "Great Pestilence" that was carving a path along the trade routes from the Near and Far East.

However early in the 1340s, The Disease had struck China, India, Persia, Syria, and Egypt. The People were scarcely equipped for the plague.
